What is DevOps?

DevOps is a set of technical concepts, tools, practices, and philosophies that improves an organization's capacity to produce high-volume applications and services, which in turn allows firms to evolve and improve products at a faster rate. Organizations can better service their clients and compete in the market because of this quickness.
